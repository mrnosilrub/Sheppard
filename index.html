A Family Legacy of Recycling Excellence

Founded by the Sheppard family in 1982, our yard has evolved from a modest rural salvage operation into Central Illinois’ go‑to source for high‑grade recycled parts and environmentally responsible vehicle disposal. Today, President Steve Sheppard leads a tight‑knit crew of professionals, while veteran salesman David Sample makes sure you get the right part at the right price.

By reinvesting in modern dismantling tech and maintaining an in‑house logistics fleet, we keep our turnaround times short and our customers smiling.
